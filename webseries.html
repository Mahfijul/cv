My Favourite Webseries


  1. The Big Bang Theory

    The Big Bang Theory is a comedy sitcom, which is about 12 sessons. It's about 4 nerdy guys and how their life change in Pasidina, California after they met Penny.
    The Big Bang Theory is available on Netflix, Prime Video.


  2. FRIENDS

    FRIENDS is an evergreen webseries. It is about Six friends living in New York City. Over the course of 10 years and seasons, these friends go through life lessons, family, love, drama, friendship, and comedy. It is a Comedy, Romance sitcom.
    FRIENDS is available on Netflix, Prime Video.


  3. Money Heist

    Money Heist is Crime TV Shows,TV Thrillers. Eight thieves take hostages and lock themselves in the Royal Mint of Spain as a criminal mastermind manipulates the police to carry out his plan.
    Money Heist is available on Netflix


  4. Little Things

    It is a comedy, romance type series. A cohabiting couple in their 20s navigate the ups and downs of work, modern-day relationships and finding themselves in contemporary Mumbai.
    Little Things is available on Netflix


  5. Kota Factory

    Dedicated to Shrimati SL Loney ji, Shri Irodov ji and Maanniya HC Verma ji, 'Kota Factory' is TVF's latest original. India's first 'Black and White' show highlights the problems present day IIT-JEE aspirants face in their day-to-day lives. It is available on Netflix


  6. Aspirants

    It is the story of the journey of three UPSC (public service competitive exams in India) aspirants and their friendship against all odds. It is available on Youtube, prime video


  7. Man with a Plan

    It is a comedy webseries. A dad finds out that parenting is harder than he thought after his wife goes back to work and he's left at home to take care of the kids. It is available on Prime Video


  8. Brooklyn Nine-Nine

    It is a comedy, crime sitcom. Comedy series following the exploits of Det. Jake Peralta and his diverse, lovable colleagues as they police the NYPD's 99th Precinct.
    It is available on Netflix.
